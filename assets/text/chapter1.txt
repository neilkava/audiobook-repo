CHAPTER 1: WHERE THE STORIES BEGIN

before ships dared to venture across the Pacific…
before the coast had borders, cities, or names written onto maps…
before the world called this land British Columbia…

there were only rain-soaked forests, restless oceans, and stories carried by the wind.

The native people who lived along these shores spoke of strange lights that appeared above the sea on certain nights.

Green lights.

Living lights.

They drifted across the sky like lanterns carried by invisible hands.

Sailors called it The Lantern Sea.

But long before science learned to explain them, sailors believed the sky itself had come alive.

Some thought the god of the sky shone lights to guide lost travellers home.

Others believed they appeared only for people searching for something they hadn’t yet found.

And far beyond the cedar forests…
beyond the cliffs and midnight black waters of the Pacific…

there was said to be an ancient ark that sailed beneath those lights.

Now most people would probably hear a story like that and think:

“Absolutely not.”

But Nom and Pom had never been very good at staying away from bad ideas.

They were world-renowned explorers.

At least according to Nom.

Together, they had survived the haunted treasure ruins of Timbuktu, discovered a magical underwater cave somewhere near the Mariana Trench, and somehow looked smokin’ through all of it.

With Nom’s skill, intellect, and extremely advanced survival instincts…
and Pom’s common sense keeping both of them alive…

they made a dangerously effective team.

They had known each other for many years.

Long enough to understand each other’s silences.

Long enough to recognize each other’s footsteps.

Long enough that even after difficult days, strange schedules, and entire oceans between them…

talking to each other still felt like coming home.

Together, they had survived difficult challenges and dangerous adventures. And somehow, through all of it, they always stood by each other.

No one understood another like Nom and Pom did.

They really were… A DREAM TEAM. The adventure duo.

Which was precisely how they ended up standing in freezing rain, nestled within a dense, ancient cedar forest in the Northwestern landmass, holding a suspicious map.
